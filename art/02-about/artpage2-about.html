ABOUT US
Who we are
We are an award-winning architectural and interior design company offering comprehensive services in architecture, interiors, 3D visualisation, bespoke furniture design and project management. We create spaces that provoke emotions. We love our work and are passionate about every project we undertake. Our facilities extend to a total of 3.500 m² of factories, workshops, and showroom exhibition space.
What we believe
Design is transformative. Design excellence produces exceptional spaces that enhance and enrich the human experience. We design to capture the imagination, lift the spirit and create spaces of lasting beauty and functionality. We believe in each other and are a team-focused company, committed to design, innovation and creativity. We believe our clients to be our most important partners.
Our mission & values
Our company mission statement sums up who we are and why we are here: “Innovative architects. Inspiring Designers. Skilled and passionate craftsmen. One company with one objective: to design, build and furnish beautifully detailed contemporary homes.” Our values are based on a positive mental attitude to life and work. We value honesty, integrity, fairness, collaboration, mutual support and respect.
Why choose UDesign?
Everything you need for designing, building, renovating or furnishing your home is in one company. Our architects, interior designers, 3D artists, furniture makers and project managers all work together for the same aim. We are renowned for our commitment to design excellence and innovation. You should come to UDesign with high expectations. We promise to be your most approachable and committed partner.
